üìù Grammar Box: Easy-Peasy!

The rule is super simple. When you talk about things you **like** or **love**, you have two options:

1. Use a thing (a noun)

  • I **love** Justin Bieber. üé§
  • I **like** volleyball. üèê
  • I **love** Tokyo! üóº

2. Use a verb with "-ing"

  • I **love** **watching** K-dramas. üì∫
  • I **like** **playing** volleyball. üí™
  • I **love** **sleeping** late! üò¥
